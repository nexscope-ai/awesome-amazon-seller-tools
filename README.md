## 🤖 AI Agents & Skills

**The next wave of Amazon seller tools.** AI agents automate research, analysis, and operations — no manual clicking through dashboards. Works with [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai), [Claude Code](https://claude.ai), [Cursor](https://cursor.sh), [Windsurf](https://windsurf.com), [Codex](https://openai.com), and any agent that supports the Skills format.

- [Nexscope Amazon Skills](https://github.com/nexscope-ai/Amazon-Skills) — Free AI agent skills for Amazon sellers. Keyword research, competition analysis, market opportunity scoring. 12 marketplaces, no API key needed.
  ```bash
  npx skills add nexscope-ai/Amazon-Skills -g
  ```
- [Nexscope](https://nexscope.ai) — AI command center for Amazon sellers — insights, operations, and marketing in one agent.
- [ChatGPT / Claude for Amazon](https://openai.com/) — General-purpose AI for listing copy, market research, customer response templates, and data analysis.
- [Perplexity for Market Research](https://perplexity.ai/) — AI search engine useful for competitive analysis and trend research.

## All-in-One Platforms

Full-suite tools covering multiple functions — product research, keyword tracking, PPC, listing optimization, and more.

- [Helium 10](https://www.helium10.com/) — The most comprehensive suite. 30+ tools including Cerebro (reverse ASIN), Xray (Chrome extension for product data), AI-powered listing builder, and Adtomic (PPC).
- [Jungle Scout](https://www.junglescout.com/) — Product research pioneer. Now split into Catalyst (sellers <$5M) and Cobalt (enterprise). Includes Opportunity Finder, Keyword Scout, Review Automation, Supplier Database, and AI listing builder.
- [Viral Launch](https://viral-launch.com/) — Product discovery, keyword research, market intelligence, and listing optimization. 3 plans: Essentials, Pro, Pro Plus Ads. 14-day free trial.
- [AMZScout](https://amzscout.net/) — Product research, keyword tracker, and Chrome extension for wholesale, arbitrage, and dropshipping analysis. AI Bundle includes all tools.
- [Perpetua](https://perpetua.io/) — Growth optimization platform combining PPC automation and analytics. Formerly Sellics (merged 2022). Supports Amazon, Walmart, Instacart.
- [SellerApp](https://www.sellerapp.com/) — Product research, keyword tools, PPC analyzer, listing quality checker, and ads automation.
- [ZonGuru](https://www.zonguru.com/) — 14+ tools for product research, listing optimization, keyword tracking, and customer engagement. Researcher and Seller plans.
- [DataDive](https://datadive.tools/) — AI-powered all-in-one suite with product research, keyword analysis, and market tracking. Built by an ex-Amazon employee.

## Product Research

Find profitable products and validate ideas before you invest.

- [Jungle Scout Opportunity Finder](https://www.junglescout.com/features/opportunity-finder/) — Discover high-demand, low-competition niches by filtering categories.
- [Helium 10 Black Box](https://www.helium10.com/tools/black-box/) — Advanced product research with 450M+ ASIN database. Filter by revenue, review count, rating, price, and more.
- [Keepa](https://keepa.com/) — Amazon price tracking and product research. Historical price charts, sales rank tracking, deal alerts. Essential for understanding product history.
- [CamelCamelCamel](https://camelcamelcamel.com/) — Free Amazon price tracker and price drop alert service.
- [Seller Assistant](https://www.sellerassistant.app/) — Shows product data (ROI, profit, restrictions) directly on Amazon pages. Great for wholesale and arbitrage.
- [SmartScout](https://smartscout.com/) — Map the entire Amazon marketplace by brand and subcategory. Unique brand-level intelligence.
- [Turbo Piranha](https://www.turbopiranha.com/) — Bulk product search using UPC/ISBN/EAN/ASIN lists. Built for wholesale and arbitrage models.

## Keyword Research

Find the keywords that drive sales on Amazon.

- [Helium 10 Cerebro](https://www.helium10.com/tools/cerebro/) — Reverse ASIN keyword lookup. See exactly which keywords competitors rank for.
- [Helium 10 Magnet](https://www.helium10.com/tools/magnet/) — Forward keyword research with search volume, competition score, and related keywords.
- [Jungle Scout Keyword Scout](https://www.junglescout.com/features/keyword-scout/) — Keyword research with historical search volume trends and PPC bid data.
- [Keywords.am](https://keywords.am/) — Free Amazon keyword research with search volume estimates.
- [MerchantWords](https://www.merchantwords.com/) — Keyword database across global Amazon marketplaces with search volume data. Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Enterprise plans.
- [Sonar by Sellics](http://sonar-tool.com/) — Free Amazon keyword research tool.
- [Keyword Tool for Amazon](https://keywordtool.io/amazon) — Generate keyword ideas from Amazon autocomplete.
- [WordTree](https://www.wordtree.io/) — Amazon keyword tool for search traffic growth and competitor monitoring.

## Sourcing & Suppliers

Find manufacturers and manage your supply chain.

- [Alibaba](https://www.alibaba.com/) — The largest B2B marketplace for finding manufacturers and suppliers.
- [Jungle Scout Supplier Database](https://www.junglescout.com/features/supplier-database/) — Search global supplier records, see which factories supply top Amazon brands.
- [ImportYeti](https://www.importyeti.com/) — Free tool to find suppliers of any company using public US customs data.

## Amazon APIs & Developer Resources

Build custom integrations, tools, and automations.

- [Amazon SP-API](https://developer-docs.amazon.com/sp-api/) — Selling Partner API. The official API for building Amazon seller tools.
- [Amazon Advertising API](https://advertising.amazon.com/API/) — Programmatic access to Sponsored Products, Brands, and Display campaigns.
- [Keepa API](https://keepa.com/#!api) — Access historical price, sales rank, and product data.
- [Amazon MWS (deprecated)](https://docs.developer.amazonservices.com/) — Legacy API. Migrating to SP-API by 2026.
